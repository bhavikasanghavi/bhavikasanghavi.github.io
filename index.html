Some words about us

‘Funway’ is inspired by a German word called ‘Fernweh’ which explains ‘A craving in a person to travel to a long distance’. We believe that everybody have a ‘Fernweh’ in them, they just need a path to express it. We at ‘Funway Travels’ provide this feeling a mode of transport.


We are based in Mumbai and. We believe in Freedom, Freedom to experience New place, New people, Diverse Culture, Different Tradition and Cuisines from all over the world. We work in a fashion to provide each client the most complete travel experience at best price.
